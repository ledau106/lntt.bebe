Welcome to the ASIAN FOOD WebQuest! This WebQuest is designed with the purpose of allowing the students to discover various food around the Asia. They will have a chance to explore interesting knowledge about food in Asia. The students will also learn about some cuisine terms and facts to expand their knowledge. What’s more, this WebQuest will help foster students’ critical thinking and creativity when they do an analysis about food and finish their final task. Description: You will create a menu for your restaurant. Level: intermediate and upper-intermediate Curriculum: Life skills/ Culture/ Careers
