Welcome to my ASIAN FOOD WebQuest! It is necessary for you to learn about the six critical components in the WebQuest: Introduction: This section provides scenarios so that you can have an overview of what the WebQuest is about. Task: This involves a detailed description of what you will have achieved by the end of the WebQuest. Process: This section gives a full description of the steps and rules you should follow in accomplishing the task. Resources: There is a list of resources that you will need in order to complete the task. Evaluation: This section consists of a rubric for evaluating your progress.
